What is unLAN?

  • Don't bring your desktop computer!
    • You may bring a laptop if you want, but, unlike past LANs, it is not a requirement.
    • At unLAN, we will be focusing more on games that don't require multiple computers.
  • What we'll be doing instead of computer games:
    • Phone games, like Drawphone, Spyfall, and Spyout
    • Split screen multiplayer games on the big TV
    • Trying out the virtual Reality with the Oculus Rift
    • Maybe we'll even venture into the world of OUTSIDE!
